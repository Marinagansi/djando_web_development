One of the easiest and best rental services we’ve found is By Rotation. By Rotation is a membership-based, peer-to-peer service, meaning that the business doesn’t actually own the stock, you sign up to loan or rent from other ‘peers’. By Rotation is still quite fresh to the rental industry, but they are taking it by storm and are one of the most popular options available. The founder, Eshita Kabra-Davies wants to aid style-savvy consumers to rethink their environmental impact.
